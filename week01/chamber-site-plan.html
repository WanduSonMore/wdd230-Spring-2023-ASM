Site Plan

Site Name:

Fort Collins Chamber of Anomalous Investigation and Study. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

 

Site Purpose:

The Chamber of Anomalous Investigation and Study is an organization dedicated to the study of anomalous events and entities. We investigate cases of these anomalies in order to research, capture, catalog, and understand these odd occurrences as well as provide counseling to those who have been traumatized by experiencing these events.

 

Scenarios:

• How do I make a report of a possible anomaly?

• What times is your office open?

• Where can I find your contact information?

 

Color Schema:

There will be three colors I will be using for my website. I will use black for the header, grey for the body, and purple for the navagation bar.

 

Typography:
